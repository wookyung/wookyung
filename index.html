COCONUT SILO

Coconut Silo is Hyundai Motor Group’s in-house startup. As a mobility startup, our solutions are mainly based on mobility.
Our goal is to make mobilities easy in developing countries near equator, starting from Vietnam, India, Brazil.

Coconut is a significant fruit that grows only in areas near the equator.
It is essential for people in countries near the equator,
who say that Coconut is a God’s gift.

Our goal is to make fabulous solutions for those countries,
which could ease the current market’s thirst as coconut does.
